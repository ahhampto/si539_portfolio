Hi! I'm Ashley.

My current goal is . . .

To gain experience in product design + development in both design and research capacities.

While I have lived in the Midwest for the past 2 1/2 years, I am originally from the Pacific Northwest.

My favorite hobbies when I lived in Washington included hiking and nature photography.

I am fluent in English and Mandarin, and I also have intermediate proficiency in Spanish.

My entire family is multilingual, which is a skill that they wanted to pass on to their children after immigrating from Taiwan.

 

I am also a huge dog person, which my 3-year-old beagle/shepherd mix can confirm.

I am passionate about supporting local animal rescues, especially since I rescued my pup from Chicago Canine Rescue in 2018.

When I take off my UX hat, you can find me evaluating wine.  I hold a Level 2 certificate from the Wine & Spirits Education Trust (WSET), and I love attending and hosting local tastings. 

My favorite grape varieties come from Spain and Italy, and my go-to red is either a Garnacha/Garnaxta or a Tempranillo.
